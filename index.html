Kingman, AZ, United States

Official Flag of United States

Kingman, Arizona is a small desert town with a surprising amount of character. I’ve lived here for two years now, and while it’s quiet, it has its charm. It sits right along historic Route 66, so we get a lot of quirky roadside attractions and travelers passing through. There’s a place downtown where you can still buy old-school root beer in glass bottles, and the sunsets here—deep orange and pink across the mountains—are unreal. Summers are hot, but the wind helps. Plus, there’s always at least one train rolling through town at any given time—you get used to the sound.
